PulseID Access Badge System

Register badges, record access, and download reports

Badge management

Register

Associate a new badge with a person. Enter the name below, then hold the badge on the reader when prompted.

Record

Record an access event. Hold the badge on the reader to log the person and timestamp.

Remove from registry

Remove the badge holder and delete all their records. Hold the badge when prompted.

Are you sure? This will remove the person from the registry and delete all their records.

Remove records for period

Delete all access records for the badge on the reader for a selected month/year. Hold the badge when prompted.

Are you sure? This will delete all records for the badge in the selected month.

Remove records before current month

Delete all access records for the badge on the reader before the current month (current month is kept). Hold the badge when prompted.

Are you sure? This will delete all records for the badge before the current month.

Report download

Download report

Generate and download the access report for the badge on the reader. Hold the employee badge when prompted. Choose full report or a specific month.
